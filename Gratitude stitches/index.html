How we began

The idea of having a women's bespoke tailors first appeared in the mind of a young woman who had for a long time searched in vain for a dress which would perfectly fit her. She decided therefore to start offering tailor made dresses for people for those in the same situation, ill served by the woman outfitters of the time. The enthusiastic response of her customers inspired the young entrepreneur to widen the services she offered to include tailor made Gown, which until then many Nigerian women had only been able to dream of.

The next step is to increase the range of products with high quality handmade shoes and tasteful women's accessories.

Our Services

We Sell

Clothing provides a hygienic barrier between a person and their environment. At Grattitude we sell good and quality cloths only. We sell all-ready-made cloths, Ankara cloths, and other cloths for both male and female at an affordable price.

We Rent

Renting services can help extend the life of clothes, provide easier access to items that might have been otherwise unavailable, and encourage people to buy less often. At Grattitude we rent cloths to people for a duration of one month at an affordable price

We customize
